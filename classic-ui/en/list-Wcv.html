9:22

9:22 is a mystery visual novel set in north america, in the year 2010.

Spencer, a college freshman and chronic insomniac, struggles to keep his new life together. On the third week of semester 1, he's all but given up on making new friends, having new experiences, and everything else the recruiter claimed he could expect. Come morning, as usual, he had overslept. The clock reads 9:22. That day, nothing seems right. Once he's finally on school grounds, however, things truly start to unravel.

Lookouts

Two lookouts meet in a desert, far from the sight of their gangs. What will become of this fateful meeting?

A short and sweet visual novel about two gay trans masc cowboys finding solace and comfort with each other while on a stakeout. Made for the Gay Western Jam hosted by Robert Yang.(Post jam update to come!)
