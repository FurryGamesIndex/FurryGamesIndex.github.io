Bitter, Sticky, Cold and Poison / 《苦》《黏》《寒》《毒》四部曲

Bitter Qixiang is a dog furry working as a detective, one day on his way home from work was robbed by a lion furry Lingzi. After subduing Lingzi, Qixiang did not arrest him, but wanted to help him get on the right track ......

Sticky Jiaoyu is an furry who is involved in drug trafficking. He once happened to pass by a hotel and became interested in the owner, Niannian, a fox furry ......

Chill The story goes back to the time when Qixiang was in the police academy and his class teacher was Jiaoyu ......

Poison The fourth installment in the series, which picks up where the previous three installments all left off.
