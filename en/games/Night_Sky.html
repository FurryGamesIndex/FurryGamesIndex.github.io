Night Sky

A boy with weird dreams, wondering why it always happens every night. No family except his big brother though they are both unrelated by blood. One day, the world had a pandemic where everyone was affected. Due to that, school interruption happened. The boy now struggles with his mysterious dreams, brother problems, new friends and best friends, and the pandemic.

The Night Sky is a visual novel about a human boy who was born in a world of furry. Follow the adventure of the main protagonist to a place where various consequences will happen by deciding the choices to be made
