Ore_no_Animism in Paradiso / おれのアニミズム～天国篇～

This is a mysterious world where Gods and the devils live.

One day a lot of bills and coins came down from the sky.
The money with a strong magical power, two Gods and two devils thought it was an abnormal situation.
They go to the Heaven to resolve the situation.

This is Touhou-like Economy Vertical STG sweeping all things with a large amount of financial resources.
