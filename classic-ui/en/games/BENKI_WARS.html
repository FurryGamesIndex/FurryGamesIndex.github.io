Benki Wars / 便機戦争

Would you like to sit on the toilet?

The game is about the battle for the unknown substance UNKO.

This is a super dynamic action shooting novel game.

The game part features fast-paced battles with the toilet, while the novel part allows you to experience life in the army with powerful furries.

You will be able to level up by raising your ERP (erotic point) gauge, which will allow you to take things to a naughty level.
