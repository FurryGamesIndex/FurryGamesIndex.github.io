Nekojishi

Although he was born within a religious family, college student Lin Tian-Liao has never thought about meeting three feline spirits. It's never bad to have three lovely guys around, but life must be going on. Knowing his humdrum life has gone forever, Liao still don't know what his future holds. However, his story with three big cats has already began. (From offical website)
