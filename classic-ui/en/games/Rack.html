Rack

Rack is a science-themed bondage game where you can perform sexual tests and experiments using a variety of toys and gadgets. As you play the game, you'll unlock new features, lab settings, and test subjects to play with.

The game features a massive variety of test subjects of all genders and species, including human characters. It also supports custom character importing, which means there are lots of user-created characters available.

You can get your own character added to the game by keeping an eye on my FurAffinity account, where I occasionally hold character auctions. If you're artistically inclined and have access to Flash Pro CS6 or higher, you can also make your own character using the template files, which are available to the public completely free-of-charge.
