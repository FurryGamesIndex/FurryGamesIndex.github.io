LUSTYRINTH

Lustyrinth
(c) 2011, 2018 Fenrir Lunaris
Powered by the O.H.R.RPG.C.E.

Having worked as a therapist for several months treating soldiers and families with Post Traumatic Stress Disorder, Lassie is in desperate need of some time off. Her girlfriend suggests a trip to a local business to get a makeover, with promises of relaxation and some time to unwind. But when she awakens to find herself stripped and alone in a dungeon of unrelenting perverts, Lassie must fight her way out and rescue her friends, or find herself the personal toy of a diabolical madman.
