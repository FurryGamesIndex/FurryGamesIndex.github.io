Primal Legends

Android
iOS

THE GAME

Theria was a peaceful place until the Stones of Power fell from the sky. Chaos spread across the world as the stones began to corrupt every animal they touched. Now it is up to the player to build an army of brave warriors to restore order to the world...

KEY FEATURES

  • INNOVATIVE CORE GAMEPLAY Easy to learn, but hard to master using a blend of both classic Match-3 and collectible card game mechanics.
  • MANY HEROES TO DISCOVER Meet the latest heroes and learn about their powers!
  • EPIC ADVENTURE Persevere through the trials of Theria in over 400 levels.
  • INCREDIBLE DUELS Challenge players from around the world in real-time PvP modes.
  • DECK BUILDING Adapt your strategy and build the strongest army. Evolve your heroes to unleash their full potential!

Blending classic match-3 mechanics, card trading and turned-based strategy combat elements, Primal Legends has players battle their way across 400 levels and real-time player vs player mode.

Discover more than 80 heroes and fight online opponents through Real-time PvP combat.

Recruit Heroes with unique abilities that can be unleashed on the battlefield with devastating results.

Assemble your dream team for every situation as some Heroes heal, boost, protect, deal direct damage or assassinate enemy’s units!

Winning requires strategic thinking and tactical know-how: you will have to find the right balance between matching minions against enemy forces and summoning Heroes’ super powers.

Cooperation with friends is key as it will allow you to complete your collection of Heroes and unlock their full potential.
