The Black Chapter

Microsoft Windows

The story of "The Black Chapter" depicts the main character, Samoan police officer Andy, who, in an accident many years ago, gained access to the Magic Eyes of Flow, which allow him to see through the workings of things.

Due to his fear of his new ability and the tragic memories that accompanied it, he has kept his ability sealed since then.

In the years that followed Andy became a detective.

After ordinary days, because of the transfer of the position, he and his predecessor in the police academy, Gray, jointly investigated a vicious serial murder case - the skinning case.

Just when life suddenly changes drastically, Andy's magic eye gradually awakens.

Skin flayers, cult rituals, aliens, spirit-recruiting spells, and a succession of bizarre events force him to face the past that he has already forgotten.

About Game

The Black Chapter is a typical text adventure game in which the player chooses certain options to advance the plot (i.e., an e-novel).

The player will play the game from the point of view of the main character "Andy".

There are three threads in the game, each of which corresponds to one of the three targets.

It is expected that the whole 6 chapters, some chapters for the common line, chapter length in more than 1 hour.
