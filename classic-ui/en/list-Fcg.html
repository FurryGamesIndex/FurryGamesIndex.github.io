About Elysium Above

Elysium Above is a Wargame developed using Unity 3D.

Game Feature

In the game, the player acts as a low level councellor in Bo, the small state by the East Sea, defending this newly recovered state against the onslaughts from enemies by excellent deployment and strategy, looking into the needs of every party and lead the story to different finales by your select.

By travelling through Bo and collecting treasures, you may strengthen your teammates. Besides, some important items may affect the finale!

The newly born Seven State Alliance is at confrontation against Lu under Hong's leading, and has been integrating more states to join it. Between the two superpower there is the main stage of our story - Bo.

Bo was destroyed in Lu's sudden attack 11 years ago, and Kui the king ended his life with fire in Wanyang Palace, while older prince Huang Zhu escaped to Hong in the south with third prince. Later Bo was all annexed by Lu. 11 years later, Huang Zhu took the chance when Lu garrison didn't aware, led an army to fight back.
