B101

B101 is a homoerotic visual novel made with Ren'Py, that puts you in place of a newly-graduated commander who gets sent to a secret base in a frozen desert. Why they've sent you there is a mystery, and it will test the loyalty you have to your nation.

This project is free to download and play, and if you're interested in supporting the content (which would help greatly with future artwork and paying for editing costs), then we would appreciate anything you can give. Our aim is to post one update at the end of each month.

This project contains situations of a M/M erotic nature, and by downloading you agree that you are over the age of 18.
