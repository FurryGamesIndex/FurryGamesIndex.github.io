LostMemories

WARNING: This game contains adult content. This game is intended for players over the age of 18.

Part of the story can be emotionally intense, and may not be for everyone. Please consider this before downloading.

Support this project on Patreon, get game updates up to a month before everyone else, and help us make this VN great!

Amidst a terrible storm, a beam of light shines down from the skies. From this light, a human appears in a world dominated by beastmen.

Plunged into an entirely different civilisation, play as yourself (Vincent standard name), as you try to unlock the layers behind your past.

Along the way, you will encounter four citizens who will help you...and possibly capture your heart:

  • Aaron, a kind hearted and loyal brown bear.
  • Hector, a bookish and timid polar bear.
  • Ryker, a gregarious and flirtatious tiger.
  • Hunter, a withdrawn and taciturn wolf.
