The Kamonegi is full of magic swords

It's a game of flirting in a flying ship while searching for magic swords.
The characters are called furry.
It is a game with strong homosexual elements.

Genre: Adventure game
Approximate play time: 3 to 5 hours.
Ending: 1 (with some variation)
Bad Ending: about 10.
Event stills: about 50 basic.
Adult Event: 4 characters x4 + about 10 others
The producer doesn't quite remember.

The protagonist does not speak at all. The story progresses according to your choices.
You can move freely around the ship, so it's up to you, the correspondent, to decide where to talk to and who to talk to and how to progress in the story.

There is an easy mode in this game.
From a number of options, you will be given a green color for what the story is going to be about.
Others will be red for bad endings and pink for adult ones.
Additionally, in Very Easy, green will be selected over time.
In this case, all you have to do is turn the mouse wheel to advance the game.
