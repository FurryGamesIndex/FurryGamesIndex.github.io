Ailuri

Microsoft Windows
Apple macOS
GNU/Linux

Ailuri is a 2D platformer, level based game with hand drawn art.

Explore levels at your own pace, survive against the dangers of the world and rescue other creatures in this heartbreaking story.

Features

  • Level based gameplay, full of puzzles, difficult obstacles and hidden secrets for players of all types to encounter.
  • Play alone or with a companion in couch co-op.
  • Travel through unique World Maps, explore vast Levels and defeat destructive Bosses.
  • Grapple, Climb, Swing, Throw - Ailuri is equipped with mystical abilities, use everything you can!
