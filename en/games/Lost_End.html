Lost End

Microsoft Windows
Android
This is a high degree of freedom game that combines text-based romance and nurturing.
Players will play the protagonist to experience a youthful college senior year.

Game Features
You can explore cafes, basketball clubs, hot springs, and other places in the game.
You can also raise the main character and write songs, work part-time, perform on the street, etc.
The game also has a large number of characters and storylines, all of which can date.
Just visit the characters in the game to send gifts and dates to improve the goodwill level!
