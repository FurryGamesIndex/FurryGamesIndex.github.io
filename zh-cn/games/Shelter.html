Shelter

Awoo, traveler! Welcome to Shelter!

It's a furry visual novel about a bunch of those medieval fantasy dogs and a human who made their home at a mysterious, gigantic metal structure filled with futuristic technology in the middle of fantasy Siberia.

The game takes place during the preparations for their version of fireworks night, by using a life-force fueled turret of mass destruction. I swear it makes sense in context!

It's nsfw and I think it'll satisfy dog-lovers. eyebrow wiggles

I've been a fan of visual novels long before I even found out about the existence of the furry fandom, so I always wanted to make one. I do pretty much everything on my own in this one: designing and drawing character sprites and background, the writing and background music. I hope to improve in all of those areas over time!

For the most up-to-date build check out: https://www.patreon.com/raus where I post like a month in advance. It also gives you an invitation to our amazing Discord den where I post extra content and we have chill times. :)
