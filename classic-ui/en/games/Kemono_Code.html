Kemono Code

Ivo, a young half-beast, half-demon youth from the Demon race, is suddenly transferred to live at an furry academy. Here is a world that is very different from the Demon race in both environment and philosophy. Will Ivo find his true love here, with the sultry discipline chairman, the slutty football player, the gold-worshipping teacher, and his shy and timid roommate?

(Translated from Chinese)
