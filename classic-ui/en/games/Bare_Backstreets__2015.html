Bare Backstreets (Legacy, 2015)

NOTE: This game has been canceled and released as-is, no further updates will be made.

Play as Vergence as he must combat waves of feisty enemies, all hampering to get a piece of his tail. We kept the three button formula while sprinkling in some added depth:

* Walk into enemies to grab them, each direction offering a unique move.
* Try different button combinations for extra attacks
* Perform Verges special attack to devastate enemies while sacrificing health
* Encounter multiple unique weapons and power-ups
* Put your dexterity to the test by mastering our sex minigame!
* Optional SFW mode for Let's Players or those who want a traditional game experience!
