A Walk Home

Microsoft Windows

A New Home. A new City. A new Job.

Finally, things seemed to go right for Kemmy. But he soon will come to find out that things in this City are not as idyllic as they seem! One night he makes his way home, blissfully unaware that Peace in this city ends with the arrival of the full moon. The locals hunger - for a boy to fuck!

Can our hero overcome the odds and make it home safely? Or will he fail and fall victim to the countless predators lurking in the shadows? Only YOU can help this foxy guy through his juicy adventures on his way home! Can you save his butt? On his Adventures, he will meet many foes, but also new friends to help him along the way.

This exciting Personal-Computer experience is powered by Unreal Engine 4! Don't miss your chance to experience the thrills of the Rape&Run game you've been waiting for!
