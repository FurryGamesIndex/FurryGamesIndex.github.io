Where The Demon Lurks

Where the Demon Lurks is a visual novel where you are in the shoes of the newest Demon Lord, ruler of all corrupted souls in the Underworld, the most feared being that commands an army of demons and other unholy spawns of the earth. All that... and you're bored by every second of it.

The Underworld isn't what it used to be. The soul-crushing-soul-torturing mega corporation is hell-bent on processing corrupted souls. Inheriting this company that practically runs itself, the Demon Lord questions his purpose.

Find out what it's like to be a reluctant ruler and meet a cast of demons, angels and other magical and not-so-magical misfits. Just what kind of a Demon Lord will you be in the end?

About the Game

Where the Demon Lurks is a mostly kinetic visual novel with a few branching parts, and some occasional fan service.

If you like the demo and want to support the game's development, follow the official Twitter page and look forward for our next update. Also, if you want to let us know how you felt about the game use the Google Document link (in game, it's the purple document thing on the bottom right that appears after you finish the demo) to answer our feedback form. Your opinions matter.

We don't have a Patreon YET, but if you like what you see and want to support us feel free to donate. Stay tuned for news of a Patreon.

Content Warning: While not explicit or NSFW, this game may contain suggestive scenarios.
