Hunt and Snare

System requirements: * 64 bit operating system * Quadcore, 2.8Ghz * 4 GB RAM * Nvidia 1060 or AMD equivalent Hunt and Snare is a Furry Third Person Shooter with a strong focus on sexual themes and character interaction. Travel between islands on a ship with your trusted ship crew, hunt rare species with a tranquilizer rifle or capture with bare paws,and sell to the highest bidder after rigorous sampling of the prey. Will your crew grow to love you as an equal ship commander, ...
