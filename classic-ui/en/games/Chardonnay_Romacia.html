Chardonnay Romacia / シャルドネロマンシア

An adult furry adventure game "Chardonnay Romacia"(Trial version).

You may change the language settings by accessing the Config menu at any time.

Languages Available: Japanese, English, and Chinese (Traditional).

  • This game is intended for adults. Do not play it if you are under the age of 18.
  • This game was developed for Windows 10 64 bits. It may not work correctly in other OS.
  • This game is still under development. All content is subject to change without notice.

We hope that you enjoy the first chapter of Chardonnay Romancia.

Feel free to discuss the contents of this trial version online. We are eager to listen to any feedback or comments you may have.
