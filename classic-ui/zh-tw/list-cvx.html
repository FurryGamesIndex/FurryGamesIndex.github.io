公雞神探 / Chicken Police

《公雞神探：鮮血染紅！》擁有複雜的審訊系統和偵探解謎玩法，是一款情節跌宕的冒險遊戲。

主要特色：

  • 驚險、豐富的故事情節，經典的黑色電影風格
  • 專業配音，長達十幾個小時的對話內容！
  • 精心打造的“多彩”世界
  • 複雜的審訊系統
  • 30多個獨特角色，都有各自的配音
  • 20多個過場動畫，震撼的黑色電影體驗！
  • 探尋大量線索、情報和可收藏的物品
  • 精美的原聲音樂

STORY

  • 一位神祕的女子……
  • 一個離奇的案件……
  • 再加上荒野世界最桀驁的警探！

來認識一下公雞神探吧

桑尼·費瑟蘭和馬蒂·麥肯基曾經是搭檔，他們造就了警探二人組“公雞神探”的傳奇。不過，時過境遷，他倆也快被時間給遺忘了。如今，桑尼被警隊勒令休了帶薪長假，整日依靠酒精來打發時間；而馬蒂則仍捨不得放下昔日明星探員的風光。他們最近一次搭檔辦案出了岔子，差點丟了性命，從此他們一拍兩散，再沒說過一句話……

現在因為一個離奇的案件，桑尼和馬蒂不得不再度聯手，而這個案子也是有史以來最讓他們摸不著頭腦的光怪陸離！

FEATURES

跌宕的劇情走向，燒腦的解迷玩法

《公雞神探》結合了視覺小說和經典冒險遊戲的主要元素，造就了一款情節紮實、對話豐富的誠意之作。遊戲中有30多個可以與之交談的角色，你必須仔仔細細地審問其中一些角色。

收集大量的線索和證據，並找出利爪市各個陰暗角色極為敏感的個人資訊，好加以利用來對付他們！

探索利爪市全城（一遍又一遍……）

可以造訪30多個場景，其中一些場景會隨著故事的發展而多次改頭換面。玩家可以多次返回之前的地點，以查詢其中隱藏的祕密、結識新角色並收集有關這個世界及其居民的資訊。

震撼的視覺體驗

《公雞神探》使用真實的照片和逼真的3D背景效果來構建遊戲，形成了一種玄妙而驚人的視覺風格，其靈感來自經典的黑色電影（如：《夜長夢多》(The Big Sleep)、《雙重賠償》(Double Indemnity)、《吉爾達》(Gilda)）和遊戲（如：《冥界狂想曲》(Grim Fandango)、《宇宙騎警》(Policenauts)、《掠奪者》(Snatcher)、《黑色洛城》(L.A.Noire)、《鳥籠事務所》(Aviary Attorney)、《神探墨菲系列》(Tex Murphy series)）。另外，本遊戲還深受幾部經典動畫（如：《紅豬》(Porco Rosso)、《了不起的狐狸爸爸》(Fantastic Mr. Fox)、《馬男波傑克》(BoJack Horseman)）的啟發。

原聲樂章為黑色的氛圍畫龍點睛，我們還請來了專業的配音工作室來為這款遊戲配音。該團隊也是《我們身邊的狼》(The Wolf Among Us)、《地平線：零之曙光》(Horizon Zero Dawn)和《戰錘：末世鼠疫2》(Warhammer: End Times - Vermintide 2)等諸多大作的配音團隊。本遊戲十幾個小時對話內容的配音工作已100％完成！

不眠不休的利爪市

“聽說，這是一座不眠之城。

也許這就是它為何總是如此古怪的原因了。”

利爪市成立的初衷，是為了打造一片掠食者和被掠食者能夠和平共榮的淨土。這是一個美好的夢想。然而，利爪市卻慢慢變成了荒野世界中最為陰暗的角落。

《公雞神探》是一部諷刺作品，它讓人回想起40年代黑色電影的情節和氛圍。而最好的黑色電影和最令人深思的偵探故事，都是在粗糲又絕望的爐火中烹製而成的，只有佐以大量憤世嫉俗的幽默感，才能激發出它最絕妙的風味。

媒體評價

FORBES: “從預告片可以看出，它黑暗的故事情節應該會很有趣。在這個遊戲型別裡，它有充分的潛質成為最獨特的作品之一。不必多說，這樣的遊戲在2020年裡唯此一款，希望它不只是與眾不同，更是出類拔萃。”

DESCTRUCTOID: “我知道我經常報道關於動物的遊戲，但是很少有能讓我十分期待的作品。聽完預告片裡的那首曲子你就懂了。各位，這可是原創音樂啊！擁有古怪主題和原創音樂的遊戲，讓我毫無招架之力。“人形動物”的新奇主題加上現場探案的視覺效果……沒錯，我徹底淪陷了。”

ROCK PAPER SHOTGUN: “非常幸運，我這雙還沒倒過時差來的眼睛發現了《公雞神探》，一場黑色電影風的偵探冒險即將上演，裡面有幾隻灰白的公雞和一隻非常善良的浣熊。這遊戲操作多半就是點啊點，換句話說：我大概率是不會去玩的。不過我還是希望你們能和我一起欣賞完這條預告片。絕對的影視大作。”

心之符 / Notes of Hearts

《Notes of Hearts》是一款研發中的PC和Mac平臺遊戲。遊戲核心玩法結合了JRPG和戀愛模擬元素，戰鬥系統則採用3v3團隊回合制的情景戰鬥模式。

遊戲的劇情一共分成6個章節，玩家在劇情裡將時刻做出選擇，而這些選擇將會對劇情的走向產生重要影響。隨著章節的推進，冒險的世界會越來越廣闊，眾多性格鮮明的角色也將陸續登場。

根據玩家的選擇，多姿多彩的角色們會隨著劇情發展加入到冒險的隊伍之中，不同的角色組合將會帶來不同的遊戲體驗。

羅曼聖誕探案集

羅曼聖誕探案集是一款讓推理迷在重溫經典的暴風雪山莊模式的同時，又不失新意的核心向本格推理遊戲。（而且角色都是獸人！）

QQ群：599175499

如果遇到卡頓，請嘗試關閉防毒軟體、檢查檔案完整性、關閉錄屏軟體、並在遊戲主選單“Language”選項內修改渲染方式。

臨近聖誕節，十三名旅客因為一場暴雪，在一間郊區的小旅館暫避。狹小的旅館裡，十三人之間的新仇舊恨一點點被激發。接連發生的案件讓旅客人人自危，作為偵探，你只有幫助旅館老闆解決這些案件，才能繼續安然度過這個聖誕節……

遊戲中一共有五個原創推理謎題，不僅可以作為單獨案件，而且案件之間都有微妙的聯絡。

太陽底下無新事，但是在太陽之國堊坡利恩 卻不同。這個有著許多種族獸人的國度與人類世界有諸多不同，而酒館正是風俗習慣的聚集地。在沒有凶殺案的閒暇時光裡，酒館也不缺乏娛樂活動！在酒館裡，你甚至可以擲飛鏢、猜花色。總之和旅客們多多交流，瞭解這個獸人世界的風俗吧。

隨著你的每個選擇，你的未來會被導向不同的境遇。如果擁有回溯時間的能力，也許能讓大家愉快地度過一個聖誕假日？

遊戲中有多種輔助推理系統，以幫助玩家解決這些謎題。

不管是人類還是獸人，偵探的推理都是建立在證物上的！在開始推理前，請務必認真搜尋酒館裡的每個角落，認真詢問每個人。

作為一名狼人偵探，羅曼有自己的種族天賦——嗅覺。我們沒有錢做那種能讓玩家嗅到現場氣息的外設，所以我們建議各位玩家買塊肉，在嗅探環節拿出來聞一聞——其實我們有其他解決方法。我們將氣味與顏色一一對應，把狼人靈敏的嗅覺視覺化，好讓玩家能感受到，並利用“嗅覺”來破案。

有時一樣證物裡有多個可疑的地方，利用塔羅牌，玩家可以一目瞭然地發現證物中可疑的地方，並作出選擇。

輪盤包含了一個案件的四要素——動機、手法、凶器和嫌疑人，依次完成輪盤的各個部分，就能接近真相！

作案的手法總是詭奇複雜，按照時間順序把發生過的事情排一排，能讓玩家理清思路。

從收集到的證物中，找到與對方證言矛盾的那個，然後帥氣地甩到他臉上！（現實生活請勿模仿）

遊戲的高潮！隨著精美的連環畫和偵探一起，完全探清案件的一切，並在途中作出選擇，補全案件的面貌。

本遊戲含輕微血腥內容（我也沒辦法去掉畢竟這是個天殺的偵探遊戲）

綠葉之冠 / The Crown of Leaves

The Crown of Leaves is a hybrid of non-kinetic visual novel and point-n-click adventure, that will tell you a story about mysticism, riddles, constellations, anthro, beasts and ghosts!

Three chapters are planned for release; The first chapter has already been released - and it's here.

There are only two people that work on the game (+ a programmer and a composer) and non of them is professional in game development or programming. This is our first game.

Plot

Roui, a resilient city dandy, a half-educated jeweler and the author of scientific magic articles, dealt with a colossal failure and had to return to his homeland: Latori. Science isn't a thing here, and local shoddy mystical beliefs are really irritating and dejecting to Roui. He dreams to return to his old life... But how can it be possible if you have no money? One day, a prosperous local baron commissioned him for a bracelet, a gift for his lovely bride. Roui, in need of the extra coin, graciously accepted the order... But not long afterward he faced a new problem - the Mad Rook, a legendary and mysterious spirit, began to meddle in the lives of the locals, including Roui's. This spirit, an entity that plays tricks on mortals once a year, is possessed with strange magic and a dark sense of humor.

Despite all the strangeness of the story, everyone can find something parallel to their everyday life and see themselves in one of the characters. This story will capture you and won't let you go until the very end. Turn your mind inside out through a twisted way!

Setting

Where is it set? The setting is a fictional world called Shang-La, a world of mixed cultures and eras, and home to various strange creatures. This is a place of old magic and new ideas, where science works together with mysticism. You can find glasses and phones, electricity and radio here, but no guns or television. The inhabitants of this world still fight with swords. The continent they live in is surrounded by the boundless ocean. The Sun and the Moon take turns alternating in the sky, and the stars are made of metal and hiding tons of secrets in them.

Who are the Sahash?

Sahash are the second intelligent race in Shang-La, alongside humans. They are divided into five nations, differing from each other by their physicality, shape of their horns, and muzzles. Every nation has its own unique traditions and culture: ranging from habits, religion, law, and even tastes in fashion and food.

Despite the complicated relationships between these ethnic groups of Sahash, as a whole they get along with humankind, and even try to emulate them in spite of their more animalistic nature.

Key features

  • Communicate with multiple characters, forge friendships, and make bitter enemies.
  • Collect and craft items!
  • Explore the world of Shang-La and meet unusual creatures, animals, spirits, and ghosts.
  • Choose who you wish to become - the hero or the villain.
  • Search for hidden clues to learn more about the game characters and plot!

In our game you will find:

  • Animated sprites
  • An original, richly-crafted world with intriguing concepts
  • Atmospheric OST
  • A few episodes of the game

The Hayseed Knight

The story of the great Knight of the Upperfield! The bravest! The most handsome! The… yeah no.

The Hayseed Knight is a plot driven fantasy comedy visual novel created by a single person (yo!) that follows the adventures of Ader, the one eyed deer, while he desperately tries to stay on track to fulfill his dream: becoming a knight!

But he's a peasant... and pretty damn poor at that! That's not going to stop him, is it? ...WELL, it kinda is. Dreams aren't edible after all.

On a meta sense, the player is The Narrator, a character hellbent on finding out what truly happened to Ader and his friends, who are little more than legendary characters at this point.

  • Fully and lovingly voice acted!
  • Gorgeous, animated sprites!
  • Meet adorable, unique characters!
  • A detailed encyclopedia for people who love worldbuilding!
  • Completely safe for work. Yes really. PG 13. Please, stop asking. Please.
  • COMPLETELY FREE. Like the wind.

This is still very much in development, so anything is subject to change in future versions! If you want to help me develop this game, there's a patreon and any contribution is really appreciated!

Winds of Change

Winds of Change is a 2D Visual Novel/Point-and-Click Adventure Game set in a living, breathing fantasy world. Embark on the journey of a lifetime! Join The Rebellion, amass an army, and liberate the world! Fully voice acted, with romance options, side quests, and optional content! Inspired by Dragon Age and Mass Effect. A huge amount of dialogue choices let you role play exactly as you want! Huge conversational trees! A game-changing 2D adventure project!

STORY

You are The Seer of Valinorth.

Blessed by the realm of spirits, you are given visions of the future in order to steer your nation's history down the right path. However, one night, a vision shakes the very foundation of everything you know. Forced to leave your home for good, you are thrust into the larger world of Alestia, and taken in by the arms of The Rebellion. Vying for freedom from the tyrannical rulers of the world – The Triumvirate -- The Rebellion fights for liberation.

At the center of this conflict is the mysterious “Blade of Exodus”. Once belonging to The Triumvirate themselves, it seems the key to victory may lie in using their own weapon against them. But who can wield an artifact of such immense power? Is victory really within reach, or will The Rebellion fall and be lost to history? The fate of everything rests on your shoulders alone, Seer.

Choose wisely, and enjoy your adventure...

FEATURES

  • In-Depth point and click interface!
  • Massive story line set in an incredible fantasy world!
  • Fully voice acted by a stellar cast!
  • Bond with your comrades!
  • Pursue friendships, and even romances! (not limited by gender)
  • Non-linear story flow! Feel free to stop and explore!
  • Open world atmosphere. Return to past areas at any time!
  • Find hidden objects and books in the environment! Learn about the world!
  • Stop and chat with your party members at any time!
  • Watch your party members interact with each other in skits!
  • View parallel scenes taking place all across the world! Multiple stories at play!
  • Experience side quests and optional stories!
