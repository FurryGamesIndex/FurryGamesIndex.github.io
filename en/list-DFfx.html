DragonBlast

What is DragonBlast? DragonBlast is an unfinished doujin fighting game for the PC that is designed to be simple, with great rewards for hard reads. Counterhits steal the opponent's meter as well as life, and are accompanied by very visible effects and slowdown whenever they occur. Matches are often very quick, and damage overall is high.
